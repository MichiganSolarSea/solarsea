Our Teams

About Our Teams

Our three sub teams work together in order to lead MSS to victory! Each team works throughout the year to prepare for our summer competition - the Promoting Electronic Propulsion Competition. We rely on communication across all three teams in order to reach our goals! We operate with the DEI initiative that states that diversity is paramount to MSS' success. We are building the next generation of leaders in the field of engineering. Everyone is welcome to our team regardless of background, experience, or area of expertise. There is a place for everyone at MSS!

*Along with our diverse makeup of majors, 54% of our members are pursuing a minor and 58% are pursuing graduate study after their undergraduate education.

Hull and Drivetrain Team

Responsible for keeping us moving forward, the Hull and Drivetrain Team combines Mechanical and Naval Engineering to keep us ahead of the competition. This team designs hull and hydrofoil structures alongside performing motor and electrical system integration. They are mainly responsible for the hydrofoils, motor, propellor, and hull.

Electrical Team

By coordinating our solar systems with a wide array of electronics, the Electric Team combines Electrical Engineering and Computer Science to help us put the pedal to the metal. They are in charge of researching and building the boat's motor and safety systems with key software controls. They are also responsible for implementing the solar array, sensors, and the battery.
