Our Preparation

Research and Design

Hover to flip ->

Research and Design are critical for growth. Without having a boat from previous years to evolve, we have the opportunity to choose a technological path. Investigating and developing technologies is key in our performance.

Data Collection

Hover to flip ->

Data Collection allows us to learn from and optimize our work. We use sensor data to track what our boat is seeing and how it is reacting. This information can then be used to further develop our software and train our pilot.

Calculations

Hover to flip ->

Calculations are the backbone of our technical progression and performance. It’s important that we can implement what we learn in class to quantify designs and program our boat to make the right moves.

Team Leadership Meetings

Hover to flip ->

Team Leadership Meetings ensure the subteams are working as a cohesive unit and give all team members the opportunity to contribute to big decisions. These open meetings also foster multidisciplinary progress.

Weekly Subteam Meetings

Hover to flip ->

Weekly Subteam Meetings keep our team members on the same page and help us plan our activities. At these meetings, project leads present their work so that our members drive developmental ideas and learn from each other.

Team Member Training

Hover to flip ->

Team Member Training is integral to ensuring that our members have the tools and knowledge necessary to succeed. We train our members in a variety of software, machinery, and programs to guarantee our members can conquer any obstacles.

Underclassmen Recruiting

Hover to flip ->

Underclassmen Recruiting gives younger students opportunities to take on leadership roles and apply their theoretical knowledge to real-world situations. These students can explore their diverse interests and find areas of specialization.

Networking

Hover to flip ->

Networking with sponsors, peers, and faculty is essential to the development MSS. We seek to foster new connections that our members can utilize to grow their knowledge base and develop relationships.

Sponsorship Outreach

Hover to flip ->

Sponsorship Outreach ensures that our team has the funds, knowledge, and support necessary to innovate throughout the year. Maintaining these relationships is vital to the continued success of our project team.
