Our History

The Electric Boat Project team at UMICH was founded by students who shared a passion for sustainability. They designed and built an eco-friendly electric boat, showcasing their determination to tackle complex engineering challenges. The team's success has inspired others to pursue similar projects, advancing sustainable marine transportation.
