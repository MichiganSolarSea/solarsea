Our Sponsors

Our project is made possible by the support of our generous sponsors. They assist Electric Boat with technical skills, knowledge, supplies, and funding to ensure we can build a competitive boat.

American Society of Naval Engineers

ASNE is the leading professional engineering society for engineers, scientists and allied professionals who conceive, design, develop, test, construct, outfit, operate and maintain complex naval and maritime ships, submarines and aircraft and their associated systems and subsystems.

FlowHy

FlowHy is researching and developing passenger boats with electric propulsion and hydrofoil technology. They are formerly known as Detroit Electric Boat, but became FlowHy after some rebranding.

Ilmor Engineering

Ilmor is a manufacturer of high-performance racing and recreational marine engines, with a drive for pushing the boundaries of innovative engineering in everything they do.

Michigan College of Engineering

We strive to anticipate the changes ahead and provide scientific and technological leadership - for the common good. Michigan Engineering is home to top-ranked departments that collaborate within the nation's number one public research institution - The University of Michigan.

How to Sponsor

Your sponsorship donations will be used in one of two ways. Monetary donations will be used for boat research and development. These funds will also be used to pay for essential tools, parts, programs, and travel to the PEP competition in Baltimore, MD. Material donations that you can provide would be extroardinarily helpful in achieving our goals. All zone memberships include perks of the zones prior as well.
